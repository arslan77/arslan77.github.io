Engineer Ships System Handling 50M+ Events Per Day at Delivery Hero

Integration infrastructure for 50+ restaurant partners in the Middle East; a technical feat praised as "the backbone of HungerStation's partner mesh"
By Technology Correspondent · Special Report

In October 2023, Mr. Arshad joined the ranks of HungerStation in Dubai, charged with the formidable task of building a universal integration layer capable of onboarding over fifty restaurant partners through a single Go-based gRPC interface.

The initiative was no small engineering feat. Partners range from local kebab shops to multinational chains, each with differing menu formats, pricing schemas, and operational cadences. Mr. Arshad's service, deployed on Google Kubernetes Engine, normalises these disparate signals into a coherent, real-time menu and order-management pipeline.

"We needed one interface to rule fifty kitchens. The gRPC contract became our constitution."

A second major initiative — Scheduled Orders — saw Mr. Arshad navigate the complex temporal constraints of Ramadan and Eid, where delivery windows shift dramatically and customer expectations are at their peak.
